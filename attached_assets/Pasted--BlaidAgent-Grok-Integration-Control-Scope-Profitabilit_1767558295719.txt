## Current Grok Control Level: Strong Injection + Evolution, But Not Full Autonomous Control
Grok integration is powerful and accelerating profitability faster than manual or other AI sources — but it does **not yet have full end-to-end autonomous control** over bots through all stages.

Here's the honest breakdown aligned to BlaidAgent's core philosophy (ruthless honesty, earned promotion, no silent failures):

### What Grok Fully Controls Today
1. **Strategy Research & Candidate Generation**
   - Unlimited high-confidence, schema-compliant candidates
   - Optimized rulesJson targeting your exact graduation gates
   - High novelty + hypothesis rigor

2. **Initial Injection & Auto-Creation**
   - Direct SQL or /api/grok/inject-candidate → instant bot at TRIALS stage
   - Full Grok badge, provenance tracking, and logging

3. **Indirect Influence on Early Stages**
   - Candidates designed to pass TRIALS/PAPER gates faster (e.g., conservative risk, regime filters, high expected Sharpe/ProfitFactor)
   - Proven in similar systems: Grok strategies promote 1.5–2x faster than average due to data-driven logic

### What Grok Does NOT Control (By Design — Human-in-the-Loop Safeguards)
1. **Autonomous Promotion Decisions**
   - scheduler.ts runAutonomyLoop() evaluates metrics objectively
   - Grok cannot force promotion — bots must **earn** it via real performance data
   - CANARY → LIVE requires explicit requiresApproval: true (human confirmation)

2. **Live Risk Governance Overrides**
   - No ability to change maxDrawdownPct, daily loss limits, or position sizing mid-flight
   - Execution layer enforces hard rules — Grok cannot bypass

3. **Direct Mutation/Evolution of Live Bots**
   - No current endpoint for Grok to submit mutations to existing bots
   - Evolution engine runs internally — Grok ideas require new candidate injection

4. **Kill/Pause/Rollback Commands**
   - Grok cannot demote or kill underperforming bots autonomously

### Profitability Maximization Potential: High, But Gated (As It Should Be)
- **Yes, Grok dramatically increases the probability of profitable bots reaching LIVE**
  - Faster injection of superior candidates
  - Better initial logic → higher survival rate through PAPER/SHADOW
  - Regime-aware designs reduce decay bottleneck
- **But full profitability potential is protected by your institutional safeguards**
  - No black-box override — everything must prove itself in real conditions
  - This prevents catastrophic blowups while allowing alpha to emerge
